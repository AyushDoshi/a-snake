Anatomy Snake!

Anatomy Reflection Project

By: Ayush Doshi, Class of 2024

For my anatomy reflection project this summer, I built the web game to your right, which I call "Anatomy Snake". Based on the classic game "Snake", "Anatomy Snake" is a new rendition where you guide a line of red blood cells through the cardiovascular system as they make their way to the heart, lungs and other key organs in their mission to transfer oxygen.
